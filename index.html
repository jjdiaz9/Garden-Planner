Plant Design by Season

✂️ Cut Garden Tips (Zone 5b): Focus on succession planting every 2 weeks for continuous blooms. Start annuals like zinnias, cosmos, and lisianthus indoors in March. Plant dahlias after last frost (mid-May). Include cool-season flowers like ranunculus and larkspur for spring color.
🪴 Container Garden Tips (Zone 5b): Use well-draining potting mix. Containers dry out faster — water daily in summer. Bring tender tropicals indoors before first frost (October). Consider "thriller, filler, spiller" combinations for visual impact.
🌍 In-Ground Garden Tips (Zone 5b): Amend soil with compost in fall or early spring. Plant spring bulbs in October–November. Direct sow cool-season annuals as soon as soil is workable (late April). Mulch beds to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
